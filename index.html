Snack Safely

A Picky Pig is a small allergy friendly bakery. Our baked goods are completely free of gluten, dairy, eggs, tree nuts, soy, peanuts, and seeds.

Most of the items on our menu are sweetended with pure maple syrup or honey and all items are baked in a 100% gluten free dedicated kitchen (happy celiacs!) with no artificial dyes ... and even some with hidden vegetables!

The Baker

Joni Trythall has been baking gluten free and allergy friendly foods since her son, who is a little ball of allergies, was born three years ago. Being diagnosed with Celiac not long after his arrival, she also completely understands the importance of food prepared in a safe, 100% gluten free environment.

She documents her recipe successes on A Picky Pig's blog and would love for you to follow along.
